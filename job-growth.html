About AI ECD Inc.

We are the team behind Kidooo AI, the AI Agent for Personalized Autism Care. Our ecosystem includes Kidooo Family (instant insights for parents) and Kidooo Clinic (AI workflow agent for therapists). Mission-First: We are deeply committed to supporting autistic children and their families. We strongly prefer candidates who have a personal connection to this mission or prior experience in the digital health space.

What You Will Build
  • A repeatable short-form content engine that drives qualified parent signups and retention.
  • A steady cadence of high-performing vertical videos (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) with weekly experimentation.
  • Trust-building content that is empathetic, non-judgmental, and privacy-aware for a health-adjacent product.

Who You Are
  • Short-form video killer: You can concept, script, shoot, and edit mobile-first vertical video fast. You understand hooks, pacing, captions, and iteration. TikTok-first mindset is a plus.
  • Proven Growth Craft: 2+ years experience driving growth through content, social, or community. You can point to outcomes — reach, engagement, signups, retention, partnerships.
  • Community Builder: You know how to earn trust, start conversations, and keep communities healthy — especially in sensitive spaces like parenting or care.
  • Early-stage fit: You move fast, work directly with founders, and thrive with high ownership and low process.
  • Local: Based in (or planning to relocate to) the San Francisco Bay Area — in-person collaboration with the founding team.
  • Sponsorship: We welcome candidates on CPT, OPT, and STEM OPT. Exceptional performers will be considered for H-1B sponsorship.
