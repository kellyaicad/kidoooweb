About AI ECD Inc.

We are the team behind Kidooo AI, the AI Agent for Personalized Autism Care. Our ecosystem includes Kidooo Family (instant insights for parents) and Kidooo Clinic (AI workflow agent for therapists). Mission-First: We are deeply committed to supporting autistic children and their families. We strongly prefer candidates who have a personal connection to this mission or prior experience in the digital health space.

What You Will Build
  • Kidooo Family core flows: onboarding, video upload, processing states, results, daily practice steps, progress tracking.
  • Trust and clarity: privacy-forward UI, calm microcopy, clear expectations and error states.
  • Design system: reusable mobile-first components that map cleanly to code and ship fast with developers.
  • Experimentation: ship improvements weekly, iterate based on real user feedback and funnel data.
Who You Are
  • Strong design taste: you consistently make crisp, modern, parent-friendly UI decisions with great hierarchy, spacing, and microcopy.
  • Fast shipper: you can go from rough concept to production-ready specs quickly, and you know when "good and shipped" beats "perfect".
  • Mission Driven: You care deeply about autism care and want your design to have a tangible social impact.
  • Self-Motivated: You thrive in an early-stage, hybrid environment where you define the solution.
  • Local: Based in (or planning to relocate to) the San Francisco Bay Area — in-person collaboration with the founding team.
  • Sponsorship: We welcome candidates on CPT, OPT, and STEM OPT. Exceptional performers will be considered for H-1B sponsorship.
