How to use Kidooo Family

Follow these steps to get your first AI summary in minutes.

1
Get Started
Create your free account
Visit family.kidooo.ai on your phone or computer. Sign up with your email — it takes less than 2 minutes. No credit card required.
2
Record
Film a 10–60 second video
Use your phone camera to capture a real moment with your child. It can be a meltdown, a win, playtime, or anything in between — there's no "wrong" clip.
Good lighting helps, but it doesn't need to be perfect
Try to capture both your child's face and their actions
Everyday moments work best — no need to "perform" for the camera
3
Upload
Upload the video to Kidooo
In the app, tap + New Moment and select your video. You can add a short note about what was happening before you upload.
4
Understand
Read your AI summary
Within minutes, Kidooo AI will generate a clear summary of the moment — including what behaviors were observed, possible triggers, and your child's emotional state — all in plain language, no jargon.
5
Take Action
Try a personalized suggestion
Kidooo will offer 2–3 evidence-informed ideas you can try with your child today. They're gentle, safe, and designed for real home situations — not a clinical setting.
6
Track & Share
Log progress & share with your care team
Over time, Kidooo builds a timeline of your child's moments. You can share selected summaries with your child's therapist or clinic — you always control what they see.
All videos are encrypted — your care team only sees the summary you choose to share
Kidooo never sells or shares your data with any third parties
Detailed Guide

Step-by-step walkthrough

Everything you need to know, from first login to sharing with your care team.

1
Account Setup
Creating your account & setting up your child's profile
1
Go to family.kidooo.ai
Open your phone browser or computer browser and navigate to family.kidooo.ai. Tap "Sign Up" to create a new account. Enter your email address and create a password. You can also sign in directly with your Google account.
2
Verify your email
Check your inbox for a verification email from Kidooo. Click the link inside to confirm your account. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.
3
Add your child's profile
On the home screen, tap "Add Your Child". Enter your child's name and birth year & month, then tap "Add Child". This creates a dedicated profile for AI insights and uploaded materials.
💡 You can add multiple children under one account. All data is kept separate and private per child.
2
Recording Tips
How to film a good video (even in chaotic moments)
1
Length: 10 to 60 seconds
Shorter clips (10–30 seconds) work great for specific moments. Longer clips (up to 60 seconds) are useful when you want to capture a full sequence, like a meltdown from trigger to resolution.
2
What to capture
Try to keep your child's face and body visible. Natural lighting works fine — you don't need special equipment. Capture real moments: transitions, mealtimes, school arrival, playtime, before-bed routines, or any behavior you've been wondering about.
3
Don't worry about being perfect
The AI works with imperfect, real-life footage. Shaky camera, background noise, or partial views are all fine. What matters most is capturing the actual behavior — not film quality.
💡 Common moments parents record: after-school transitions, morning routines, mealtime behaviors, sibling interactions, reactions to "no", and sensory play.
3
Uploading
How to upload a video and add context
1
Tap "Upload Video" on the home screen
From the home screen, tap the yellow "Upload Video" button. Select a video from your camera roll. You'll see a confirmation screen with the file name — tap "Upload" to send it for AI analysis. Tips for best results are shown on screen.
2
AI is analyzing your video
After uploading, the AI will begin analyzing the video. You can leave this page — a notification will appear on the home screen once the report is ready. The analysis typically takes 1–3 minutes.
3
Read your report
Once ready, the report shows a Development Snapshot, Communication, Social Interaction, Emotional Regulation, Learning Adaptation, and Current Challenges — all in plain, jargon-free language.
💡 Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI (most phone videos). Max duration: 1 minute. Larger files may take longer to process.
4
Action Plan
Your personalized To-Do list
1
Review "Key Actions You Can Take"
After a video report is generated, scroll down to see "Key Actions You Can Take" — a personalized list of strategies tailored to your child's observed behaviors. Each suggestion includes a brief explanation and a "Why this helps" section.
2
Add to your To-Do list
Tap "+ Add to My To-Dos" on any suggestion to save it. Added items will appear on the To-Do page where you can track Pending and Completed tasks. This keeps all your action items organized in one place.
3
Track your progress
On the To-Do page, switch between All / Pending / Completed to review your tasks. Mark items as done when you've tried them. Over time, this builds a record of what strategies you've explored for your child.
💡 Kidooo is not a medical device and does not provide diagnoses. Use these suggestions as practical starting points — always rely on your own judgment and consult your care team for clinical decisions.
5
Evaluation Report
Upload your child's evaluation report for AI summary
1
Tap "Upload Evaluation Report" on the home page
On the home screen, tap "Upload Evaluation Report". A dialog will open — tap to select your file (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOC, TXT, max 25MB). After selecting, tap "Upload" to send it for AI analysis. The AI will process the document and generate a structured summary.
2
View the AI summary of the report
Once processed, the AI generates a plain-language summary of the evaluation. To find it: go to Profile → choose your child → "View Profile" → scroll to "Uploaded Documents" at the bottom. Tap the document to read the full AI summary.
💡 You can upload IEPs, clinical assessments, OT/speech evaluations, and other therapy documents. The AI summarizes key findings so you can quickly understand what the report says without wading through clinical jargon.
6
Profile & Import
View your child's profile & import from AI tools
1
Open your child's profile
Go to the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen. Find your child's name and tap "View Profile" on the right. You can also tap your child's name to open a quick menu with options: View Profile, Upload Evaluation Report, Upload Materials, Import from AI, and Delete Profile.
2
Read the development snapshot & history
The profile page shows a Development Snapshot (Communication, Social Interaction, Emotional Regulation, Learning Adaptation), Current Challenges, and Near-Term Outlook. Scroll to the bottom to see Uploaded Documents — all evaluation reports and their AI summaries.
3
Import from AI (ChatGPT, etc.)
On the home screen, tap "Import from AI", then tap "Copy Prompt". Paste the prompt into ChatGPT or another AI tool where you've stored your child's information. Copy the AI's response and paste it back into Kidooo to generate a profile instantly.
💡 All videos and documents are encrypted at rest and in transit. Kidooo never sells your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Common questions

Is Kidooo a medical or diagnostic tool?
No. Kidooo is not a medical device and does not provide diagnoses. It is an AI-powered support tool that helps families observe, understand, and document their child's behaviors at home. Always consult a licensed professional for medical or clinical decisions.
How is my child's video kept private?
All videos are end-to-end encrypted and stored securely. Kidooo never sells your data. You choose exactly what to share with your child's care team — they only see the text summary you explicitly share, never the raw video unless you allow it.
What kind of videos should I record?
Any real moment works — a meltdown after school, a breakthrough during play, a difficult bedtime, or a happy interaction with a sibling. The AI works best with natural, unstaged moments captured in everyday settings.
Is Kidooo Family free to use?
Yes — you can start for free with no credit card required. Visit family.kidooo.ai to create your account and upload your first video.
Do I need to be diagnosed with autism to use Kidooo?
No. Kidooo is built for any family navigating neurodevelopmental challenges — whether your child has a formal diagnosis or you're in the process of seeking one. The tool is most useful for families who want to better understand and document their child's daily experiences.
