Record

Detailed Guide

Step-by-step walkthrough

Everything you need to know, from first login to sharing with your care team.

1
Account Setup
Creating your account & setting up your child's profile
1
Go to family.kidooo.ai
Open your phone browser or computer browser and navigate to family.kidooo.ai. Tap "Sign Up" to create a new account. Enter your email address and create a password.
2
Verify your email
Check your inbox for a verification email from Kidooo. Click the link inside to confirm your account. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.
3
Create your child's profile
After logging in, tap "Add Child". Enter your child's first name (or a nickname), date of birth, and any notes about their current goals or support needs. This information helps Kidooo personalize the AI insights for your child specifically.
💡 You can add multiple children under one account. All data is kept separate and private per child.
2
Recording Tips
How to film a good video (even in chaotic moments)
1
Length: 10 to 60 seconds
Shorter clips (10–30 seconds) work great for specific moments. Longer clips (up to 60 seconds) are useful when you want to capture a full sequence, like a meltdown from trigger to resolution.
2
What to capture
Try to keep your child's face and body visible. Natural lighting works fine — you don't need special equipment. Capture real moments: transitions, mealtimes, school arrival, playtime, before-bed routines, or any behavior you've been wondering about.
3
Don't worry about being perfect
The AI works with imperfect, real-life footage. Shaky camera, background noise, or partial views are all fine. What matters most is capturing the actual behavior — not film quality.
💡 Common moments parents record: after-school transitions, morning routines, mealtime behaviors, sibling interactions, reactions to "no", and sensory play.
3
Uploading
How to upload a video and add context
1
Tap "+ New Moment"
From the home screen, tap the "+ New Moment" button. Select the video from your camera roll, or record directly from the app.
2
Add a context note (optional but helpful)
Before submitting, you can add a short note about what was happening: "He hadn't napped and we asked him to stop the iPad" or "This happens every Tuesday after speech therapy." Context makes the AI summary more accurate and useful.
3
Wait for processing
Processing typically takes 1–3 minutes. You'll receive a notification when your summary is ready. You can close the app and come back — it'll be there waiting for you.
💡 Supported formats: MP4, MOV (most phone videos). Maximum file size: 500MB. If your video is too long, trim it to under 60 seconds before uploading.
4
Reading Results
Understanding your AI summary
1
What the summary includes
Each summary includes: Observed behaviors (what happened), Possible triggers (what may have caused it), Emotional state (how your child seemed to be feeling), and Patterns (if this matches previous moments).
2
Personalized suggestions
Below the summary, you'll see 2–3 gentle, evidence-informed ideas to try. These are practical things you can do at home today — not clinical prescriptions. They're designed to be realistic for busy parents.
3
If the summary seems off
AI can make mistakes. If a summary doesn't feel right, you can add a correction note or flag it. Your feedback helps the system improve over time. Always use your own judgment as the parent who knows your child best.
💡 Kidooo is not a medical device and does not provide diagnoses. Use summaries as a starting point for conversations with your care team — not as clinical conclusions.
5
Sharing
Sharing summaries with your care team
1
Select which summaries to share
On any moment's summary page, tap "Share with Care Team". You control which summaries are shared — your care team never sees your raw video unless you explicitly allow it.
2
Add your therapist or doctor
Go to Settings → Care Team and enter your care provider's email. They'll receive an invite to view only the summaries you've chosen to share. You can revoke access at any time.
3
Export for appointments
Before a therapy or doctor's appointment, you can export a PDF report of recent moments to bring with you. Tap "Export Report" from your child's timeline — it summarizes recent observations in a format clinicians can read quickly.
💡 Many parents find it helpful to share 2–3 moments from the past week before each therapy session. It gives therapists real context from home, not just what they observe in clinic.
6
Account & Privacy
Managing your account, data, and privacy settings
1
Delete a video or moment
Go to the moment, tap the three-dot menu (⋯), and select "Delete". This permanently removes the video and its summary from Kidooo's servers.
2
Change your password or email
Go to Settings → Account. You can update your email or reset your password at any time. We recommend using a strong, unique password and enabling two-factor authentication if available.
3
Request full data deletion
To permanently delete your account and all associated data, email info@aiecd.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request and confirm deletion within a reasonable timeframe.
💡 All videos are encrypted at rest and in transit. Kidooo never sells your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Common questions

Is Kidooo a medical or diagnostic tool?
No. Kidooo is not a medical device and does not provide diagnoses. It is an AI-powered support tool that helps families observe, understand, and document their child's behaviors at home. Always consult a licensed professional for medical or clinical decisions.
How is my child's video kept private?
All videos are end-to-end encrypted and stored securely. Kidooo never sells your data. You choose exactly what to share with your child's care team — they only see the text summary you explicitly share, never the raw video unless you allow it.
What kind of videos should I record?
Any real moment works — a meltdown after school, a breakthrough during play, a difficult bedtime, or a happy interaction with a sibling. The AI works best with natural, unstaged moments captured in everyday settings.
Is Kidooo Family free to use?
Yes — you can start for free with no credit card required. Visit family.kidooo.ai to create your account and upload your first video.
Do I need to be diagnosed with autism to use Kidooo?
No. Kidooo is built for any family navigating neurodevelopmental challenges — whether your child has a formal diagnosis or you're in the process of seeking one. The tool is most useful for families who want to better understand and document their child's daily experiences.
